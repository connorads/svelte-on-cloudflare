Pages vs Workers
---
<!-- alignment: center -->
<!-- column_layout: [10, 1, 10] -->
<!-- column: 0 -->
Pages
***
❌ For static sites
<!-- new_line -->
❌ No observability 
<!-- new_line -->
❌ Limited flexibility
<!-- column: 2 -->
Workers
***
✅ Serverless compute
<!-- new_line -->
✅ Logs with logpush
<!-- new_line -->
✅ Easy to add KV, D1, Durable Objects
